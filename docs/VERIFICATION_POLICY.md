# Verification policy

Publication requires: HTTPS; no credentials/custom port; exact trusted hostname;
every redirect trusted; size/content-type limits; official HTML/PDF; field evidence;
supported values; explicit geographic eligibility; and valid deadline state.

West Bengal-only, explicitly all-India, and explicitly other-state-open-to-all notices
may publish. A language requirement may publish only when geography otherwise permits
it and the condition is prominently shown. Unclear, institution-specific or unresolved
district scope goes to review. Other-state domicile-only and irrelevant notices are rejected.

“Indian citizen” alone never proves all-India eligibility. Aggregator content never
proves dates, eligibility, vacancies, links or changes. Deadline extensions,
cancellations and corrigenda need official evidence and create a revision.

AI returns schema-validated JSON at temperature zero. Deterministic checks remain the
authority; a score cannot override a failed hard gate. Public posts show verification
time and link back to the official source.
